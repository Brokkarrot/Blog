Winter is Coming!

Winter is just around the corner. If you have a wood stove like I do, you may want to figure out how much wood to stock up on before it's too late. One of the ways I make sure I'm well stocked up is to call my local wood guy. He's always reliable to fill my shed up with wood for the winter. Another way I stock up is by chopping up fallen trees on my forrest travels. Folks can often find wood on the side of the road by neighbors who are selling it or simply giving it away. Here are some other ways you can find wood for the winter:

  • Trade with a friend or Neighbor
  • Hike by a logging field
  • Steal It
And there you have it folks. Soon youll be well prepared for the coming winter. Make sure youre keeping bee-sy for the coming weather! Till next time.
